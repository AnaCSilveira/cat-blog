Are cats astronauts?

Cats are fascinating animals and surrounded by mysteries. In independent and mysterious temperament, these felines are known for their agility, elegance and behavior many sometimes unpredictable. However, recently, a theory has gained space on the internet and among conspiracy theorists: these would be the astronaut cats?

The theory suggests that cats are not land animals, but rather extraterrestrial beings sent to carry out a specific mission on our planet. There are historical reports that point to the fact that that cats were considered sacred beings in different cultures and even associated with divine figures. that could indicate that cats have a much more mystical origin than previously thought.

Furthermore, there are theorists who believe that felines have telepathic abilities and are able to communicate with others alien species. This belief is based on reports from people who claim to have seen cats reacting to stimuli that are not perceptible to human beings, or even behaving differently unusual way during astronomical events, such as an aurora boreal. These and other signs were interpreted as signs of that cats would be interplanetary beings.
